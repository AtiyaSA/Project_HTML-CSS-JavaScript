About us:

We at Otaku India want to make anime available easily to our fellow Indian Otakus. Thus, here we get the DVDs directly from Japan and add subtitle to it. Although these are not the official subtitles, they still let you contribute to your favorite creators. We are a small group of people working hard on these projects and hence, have a very limited collection.
